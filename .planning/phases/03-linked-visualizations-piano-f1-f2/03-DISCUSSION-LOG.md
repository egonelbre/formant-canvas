## Decisions Made

### F1/F2 Chart Orientation & Axes
- **Options presented:** Standard phonetics (inverted), Cartesian (natural), Standard with Hz labels
- **Chosen:** Cartesian (natural) — F1 up, F2 right
- **Rationale:** More intuitive for non-phoneticians

### Piano Harmonics Display
- **Octave range options:** 3, 5, or 7 octaves
- **Chosen:** 5 octaves (C2-B6)
- **Amplitude display options:** Vertical bars on keys, color intensity dots, colored key fill
- **Chosen:** Vertical bars above keys (user specified "above" not "on")

### Vowel Preset Interaction
- **Options presented:** Clickable labels on chart, chip buttons below, both
- **Chosen:** Clickable labels on chart only — no separate chip row

### Drag Behavior
- **Handle options:** Circle with crosshair, simple dot, dot with readout
- **Chosen:** Simple dot (~16px accent circle, no crosshair, no floating readout)
